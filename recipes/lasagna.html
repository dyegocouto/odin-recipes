Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is the ultimate comfort food, layering rich flavors in a beautifully baked dish. A combination of tender pasta sheets, savory meat sauce, and melted cheese creates a hearty meal that is perfect for family gatherings or cozy nights in.

Each bite delivers a balance of robust tomato sauce, creamy ricotta, and perfectly seasoned ground beef or vegetables. Whether served fresh out of the oven or as leftovers, lasagna is a dish that only gets better with time.

Ingredients

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 500g ground beef (or a vegetarian alternative)
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 800g canned tomato sauce
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 250g ricotta cheese
  • 250g mozzarella cheese, shredded
  • 50g Parmesan cheese, grated
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 180°C (350°F).
  2. Cook the lasagna noodles according to package instructions, then drain.
  3. In a pan, sauté the onion and garlic until fragrant. Add ground beef and cook until browned.
  4. Stir in tomato sauce, basil, oregano, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 10 minutes.
  5. Spread a thin layer of sauce on the bottom of a baking dish. Layer noodles, ricotta, meat sauce, and mozzarella. Repeat until ingredients are used up.
  6. Top with Parmesan and bake for 40 minutes, or until golden and bubbly.
  7. Let rest for 10 minutes before slicing and serving.
